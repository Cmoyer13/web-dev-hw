The Maggiore Group creates and operates high quality, creative culinary concepts across Arizona. The restaurants provide superior customer service as well as cuisine that is forward thinking. The award-winning and nationally-recognized Maggiore Group was co-founded by husband & wife duo Celebrity Chef Joey & Cristina Maggiore and partner Flora Tersigni. The co-founders have grown to a leadership team that perfects every aspect of each concept including restaurant design, music selection, culinary creations, bar program and hospitable service. The group has over seven concepts that specialize in a variety of food and beverage experiences including traditional Italian cuisine, a build-your-own Bloody Mary bar and create-your-own cannoli counter. Top-quality food, a warm and social ambiance, great customer service and products that reflect the highest standards are what fuels The Maggiore Group.
