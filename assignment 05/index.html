Chicken Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • Chicken breasts
  • Frozen peas and carrots
  • Green onions
  • Garlic
  • Eggs
  • Cooked Rice
  • Low-sodium soy sauce

Directions

  1. First, add the oils and diced chicken to a large skillet. Cook the chicken over medium-high heat for about 5 minutes, or until no longer pink inside. Remove the chicken and set it aside on a plate while you stir-fry the veggies in the same pan.
  2. To the same skillet, add the peas, carrots, and gree onions. Cook for about 2 minutes. Once softened, add the garlic and stir for about 1 minute.
  3. Put the veggies to one side of the skillet, and scramble the eggs on the other side. Add the chicken bac to the pan, followed by the rice.
  4. Drizzle eveything with soy auce and cook for about 2 minutes or until everything is heated through.
  5. Serve!

NOTES: This Recipe is best warm and fresh. It will stay airtight in the fridge up to 5 days and up to 4 months in the freezer. Reheat as desired.
